GoKu

ABOUT HISTORY

Goku’s Family:

  • Father: Bardock (Saiyan warrior)
  • Mother: Gine (Saiyan woman)
  • Grandfather: Son Gohan (raised Goku on Earth)
  • Brother: Raditz (Saiyan warrior, Goku’s older brother)
  • Wife: Chi-Chi (human from Earth)
  • Children
    • Son Gohan (oldest son)
    • Son Goten (youngest son)

Other Important Relatives:

  • Vegeta (Saiyan prince, Goku’s rival and later ally)
  • Bulma (friend and ally, often like family)

Birth and Early Life:

  • Goku was sent to Earth as a baby shortly before the destruction of Planet Vegeta by Frieza. His mission was originally to destroy Earth, but after suffering a head injury during his crash landing, Goku lost his aggressive nature and grew up as a kind-hearted, carefree boy.
  • He was raised by Grandpa Gohan on Earth, who gave him the name Goku.

Vegeta

ABOUT HISTORY

Vegeta's Birth:

  • Born: Age 732 (in the Dragon Ball timeline).
  • Birth Name: Vegeta (often referred to as Vegeta IV, as he is the prince of the Saiyan race).
  • Place of Birth: Planet Vegeta, the home planet of the Saiyan race.
  • Parents:
    • Father: King Vegeta (the ruler of the Saiyan race, often shown as a proud and powerful warrior).
    • Mother: Not much is known about Vegeta’s mother in the series.

Relationship with Goku:

  • Initial Rivalry:When Vegeta first meets Goku, he sees him as a rival and feels threatened by Goku’s growing power. He is prideful and obsessed with proving his superiority over Goku, especially after Goku becomes a Super Saiyan before him.
  • Competition and Respect:Over time, Vegeta realizes that Goku’s power keeps surpassing his own, which fuels both his rivalry and a grudging respect.
  • Ally and Partner:After the Majin Buu Saga, Vegeta and Goku become allies. They fight side by side in various battles, and Vegeta begins to understand the value of teamwork and friendship.

Birth and Early Life:

  • Goku was sent to Earth as a baby shortly before the destruction of Planet Vegeta by Frieza. His mission was originally to destroy Earth, but after suffering a head injury during his crash landing, Goku lost his aggressive nature and grew up as a kind-hearted, carefree boy.
  • He was raised by Grandpa Gohan on Earth, who gave him the name Goku.
  • Final Acceptance: While Vegeta still wants to be the strongest, he comes to accept that Goku is his equal and that their bond is not just about rivalry, but also mutual respect and friendship.
